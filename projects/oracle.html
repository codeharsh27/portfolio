The Problem

Gym owners often struggle with disjointed software for managing members, billing, and trainers, leading to administrative chaos. On the flip side, users lack a cohesive platform to track workouts, diet, and progress while staying connected with their trainers.

The Solution

FitConnect is a comprehensive SaaS Ecosystem that bridges the gap. It offers dedicated interfaces for Users (Fitness tracking), Trainers (Client Management), and Admins (Business Operations), all unified by a robust Firebase backend.

Key Features

3-in-1 Platform

Unified ecosystem for Users, Trainers, and Gym Admins.

Workout & Diet

Personalized plans, video demos, and macro tracking.

Razorpay Integration

Automated billing, subscription management, and trainer payouts.

Client Mgmt

Trainers can track client progress, schedule sessions, and chat in real-time.

SaaS Architecture

Built on Firebase for high scalability. It leverages Cloud Functions for event-driven logic (e.g., generating invoices on subscription renewal). The app follows a Provider based state management approach ensuring performance across complex dashboards.
