The Problem

73% of college students have no savings plan, and traditional finance education is often theoretical and boring. Students fear making costly investing mistakes and lack a safe environment to practice.

The Solution

Muscle Money gamifies financial literacy with a "Learn by Doing" philosophy. It combines Smart Auto-Save (automated micro-savings) with a Risk-Free Simulator for trading stocks and gold. By integrating AI-Powered Learning, it transforms complex financial concepts into bite-sized, addictive daily lessons.

Key Features

Smart Auto-Save

Automatically deducts a % of spendings and saves it via PhonePe integration.

Market Simulator

Trade stocks & gold with ₹1L dummy money using real-time market data.

AI Tutor

Personalized learning paths with quizzes adapted to your financial goals.

Gamification

Daily streaks, badges, and rewards to build consistent financial habits.

System Architecture

The platform is built on a Microservices Architecture to ensure scalability. The API Gateway orchestrates traffic between the User, Learning, and Finance services. Redis is used for caching session data and real-time market tickers, while PostgreSQL handles transactional integrity.

Tools Used

Flutter Node.js PostgreSQL Redis PhonePe API
